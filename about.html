About StepStyle

Discover the story behind our passion for luxury footwear

Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1987 by visionary designer Marco Rossi, StepStyle began as a small atelier in the heart of Milan. What started as a passion project has grown into one of the world's most prestigious luxury footwear brands.

Our founder's philosophy was simple yet profound: create footwear that transcends mere fashion to become wearable art. Each pair of StepStyle shoes represents over 200 hours of meticulous craftsmanship, combining traditional Italian techniques with innovative design.

Our Journey

35 Years of Excellence

Key milestones in our journey to becoming a global luxury brand

1987

Foundation

Marco Rossi establishes StepStyle in a small Milan atelier with three master craftsmen.

1995

First Collection

Launch of our signature Classic Collection, establishing our reputation for timeless design.

2005

International Expansion

Opening of our first flagship store in Paris, marking our entry into international markets.

2015

Sustainability Initiative

Launch of our comprehensive sustainability program, committing to eco-friendly practices.

2022

Digital Innovation

Introduction of our online platform, making our luxury footwear accessible worldwide.
