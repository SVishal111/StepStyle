Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do I find my correct shoe size?

We recommend using our comprehensive size guide, which includes detailed measurements and fitting instructions. You can also visit any of our stores for a professional fitting.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn shoes in their original packaging. Returns are free for customers in the continental US and Europe.

How do I care for my StepStyle shoes?

Each pair comes with detailed care instructions. We recommend regular cleaning with our specially formulated leather care products and professional maintenance every 6-12 months.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. All international orders include tracking and insurance.

Can I customize my shoes?

We offer a bespoke service for select styles, allowing you to choose materials, colors, and details. Contact our concierge service for more information.

What makes StepStyle shoes different?

Our shoes are handcrafted by master artisans using traditional techniques and the world's finest materials. Each pair represents over 200 hours of meticulous craftsmanship.
